Delete Your OMR Account

To permanently delete your user data from the OMR app, follow the steps below:

  1. Open the OMR application and ensure you are logged in.
  2. Navigate to the Settings screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap the Delete Account option.
  4. A confirmation dialog will appear with a warning message.
    Warning:
    Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All associated user data will be permanently removed.
  5. Tap Confirm or Delete to permanently delete your account and user data.
Note: Once your account has been deleted, it cannot be recovered.
