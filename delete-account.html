Request Account Deletion

If you would like to permanently delete your account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Open the app.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your request.

If you cannot access the app, you may also contact us at:

support@example.com

What data is deleted

  • Your account profile
  • Your login credentials
  • Any user-generated content stored on our servers

Data we may retain

Certain information may be retained for legal, security, fraud prevention, or regulatory compliance purposes for up to 30 days after your deletion request.

Processing time

Deletion requests are normally completed within 30 days.
